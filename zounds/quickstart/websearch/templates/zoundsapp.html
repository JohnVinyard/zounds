About

This audio search was built using the Zounds Python library. Queries are drawn, at random, from segments of sound in the database. Search results not only tell you which sounds are relevant to the query, but where in those sounds the relevant portions occur.

All the sounds here are from the incredible Freesound.org. If you'd like to know more about a sound, or its author, just click the "attribution" link below each sound snippet.
